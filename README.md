## 👋 About Me

I am driven by a passion for building robust, scalable applications that solve complex problems through meticulous engineering and intuitive design. My technical expertise spans the entire development lifecycle:

- 💻 **Frontend:** I craft highly responsive, premium user interfaces utilizing modern **JavaScript (ES6+)**, **Tailwind CSS**, and **HTML5**.
- ⚙️ **Backend:** I leverage **Python** for powerful automation and data pipelines, alongside **Supabase (PostgreSQL, Edge Functions, Auth)** to deploy secure, real-time serverless architectures.
- 🚀 **Infrastructure:** I actively engineer solutions integrating complex automations, specifically utilizing **Cloudflare Pages**, **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**, and **Google Apps Script** to dramatically increase workflow efficiency.

Whether I am building a clinical intake portal or a serverless proxy network, my ultimate goal is to deliver technical excellence and unparalleled user experiences.
